## Business context

Employees are the engine behind any successful business. Productivity at one level compounds across the rest of the organization. When someone works more effectively, it creates leverage. That leverage grows. Soon it becomes the difference between keeping pace and leaping ahead.

Artificial intelligence and machine learning offer a way to unlock that leverage. Not by replacing people, but by revealing insights. These tools help identify patterns, spotlight growth areas, and speed up decisions. When implemented well, they change how companies think about evaluation, development, and hiring.

Goldman Sachs launched an internal AI platform in 2024. It includes tools for summarizing investment research, translating documents, generating code, and creating charts. One standout is Translate AI. Before it launched, teams had to send documents to human translation vendors. Now, employees can drop a document into the platform and get instant multilingual output. That time savings adds up.
